Stars Die

Stars die too,
Have you ever looked up into the night
Where shadows and bulb dance their eternal linear dance As the void bathes you entirely with its light,
And when you peer, do you see their shape?
Do you press your ear close to hear their songs echo upon all?
Do you understand it as a gift?
These celestial bodies give their shape to you in their life and even more as they explode beyond their definition
completely showering our world with ghost-light
I used to see this as the finale
As the end of ends
The point where spectrum glows dark and cold
And life ceases to give meaning or add value to final sum
But seeing so many local star points go out so quickly in their youth I beg to know the meaning.. Of why and how come? Where are we going?

miti.
